eirdest encounter of my FUCKING life just happened yesterday.
So, It's CNY and you know, got myself a 5 days holiday, might as well take a trip home.
Took Grab to the nearest bus station, realized I went there 3 hours early, fuck.
With 3 hours to spare, I started playing with my phone and yknow starts messing around with Youtube, Spotify, Games, the whole nine yards, I was bored basically.
Around 20 minutes mark, I started playing [PJSekai], the [Chunithm-lookalike] rhythm game, shits fun you should try it.
Anyway, I played the shit for like a good 10 minutes or so and this girl just came out of fucking nowhere, looking all shy.

AND I shit you not she was doing that anime shy pose:

I was so fucking bewildered by this, but didnt really say anything, then the conversation goes something like this: 

<Her>: Hey, mind if I ask what time your bus arrives?
<Me> : Uhhhhh, lemme check. 10:30. (Not sure why I would tell her that, I wasn't thinking.)
<Her>: Ah... then, would you mind following me for a bit?
<Me> : EH?? What? (At this point, I thought I was getting scammed.)
<Her>: Oh no, I noticed that you were playing Project Sekai and I'm by myself. 
       It would be really nice to have some company. I'm a girl, and it's night, so...
<Me> : Oh, I understand. Sure, sure, I don't mind. 
       (Again, wasn't thinking, but my scam detector isn't going off so it's probably fine.)

Then I did exactly that, just chatting with her and stuff, it turns out she came with 4 people (friends I assumed), 1 female and 3 male, the one girl already got on her bus, the 3 other dude went out vaping or whatever the fuck, leaving her alone.
So she was bored as fuck, I assumed, hence the encounter. It was really nice though. We didnt really exchange our name; just going by "you and i" basis the whole time, its not awkward or anything like that though, it was fun actually.
By some dumb luck, we kinda have a lot in common (in games that is), so lotsa conversation about rhythm game, sdvx, maimai, the usual. She also went to the same uni as mine, different major though. Still cool.
Anyway, to you, the random girl, thanks for making my life a little more bearable, I didnt know I could have this much fun with a stranger.
